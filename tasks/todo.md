# Lunatik - Endless Runner iPhone Game

## Game Concept
Side-scrolling endless runner starring Luna (black & white border collie mix).
Inspired by Sonic and Subway Surfers. Luna dashes through a neighborhood
collecting pizza slices, bones, and tennis balls while dodging obstacles.
